About pyBreak

Tennis stats analysis

This app aims to present statistical data, which describes performance of the best tennis players ranked in TOP 200 of ATP Ranking. Data is being scraped from chosen reliable data sources with use of programming techniques and it is up-to-date. The following section shows two modules of pyBreak, divided into dynamic analysis of each player's game statistics with possibility of direct comparison with other players, and summaries, which distinguish the best players in particular tennis areas.

Odds statistics

The extra feature of that statistical app is that it contains also the stats which describes players performance depending on the pre-match odds. We can see, how each player in the database performs in different scenarios - whether he is favored by the role of match favorite, or is it likely, that the player surprises as an underdog.

pyBreak Content

Players Stats

Presentation of data regarding performance of each player ranked in TOP 200 of ATP Ranking. Main page of this site contains the tables showing stats of all players from up-to-date ranking in areas of serve, return and bookmakers' odds scenarios.

You can choose or search for your player, to enter his profile in the database with some nice graphic charts, that show player's performance.

Each player's profile includes options of data filtering in terms of period of time and court surface. You can not only view the player's stats graphics, but also compare him with any other player from the base and see who plays better in particular period or surface.

DATA SOURCES


Source of matches data for all the players who compete in professional tennis tournaments. Website lead by Jeff Sackmann - great data analyst and tennis enthusiast, contains up-to-date statistics related with current and historical matches from ATP Tour and ATP Challenger Tour competitions. Besides that, author conducts very interesting own analysis, f.ex. his own idea's Elo Ranking, which ranks players according to their performance. There are also probability predictions of the chances of particular player participating in the current tournaments.


Website containing huge base of historical bookmakers' odds for all professional matches, that have taken place over many of the last years. It was crucial task to get the data of same matches that were included in the statistics from TennisAbstract site, and match them in such a way as to get the possibility to present the analysis of each player's performance depending on the pre-match scenario. This website is great source of stats for everyone who likes to bet on sportsbooks.
